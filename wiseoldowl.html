The Wise old Owl

$2.00

The Wise Old Owl is a classic fable that teaches the value of wisdom and silence. The story revolves around an old owl that observes everything happening around it but speaks very little. Instead of talking unnecessarily, the owl listens carefully and learns from what it sees and hears. Because of its patience and observation, the owl becomes wiser over time. The moral of the story is that those who listen more and speak less gain greater knowledge and understanding. This teaches us the importance of being attentive, thinking before speaking, and valuing wisdom over unnecessary chatter.
