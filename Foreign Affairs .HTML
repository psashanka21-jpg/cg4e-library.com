About Magazine

Foreign Affairs is a prestigious American journal focusing on global politics, international relations, and economic policy. Published by the Council on Foreign Relations (CFR) since 1922, it is known for its thought-provoking essays, analysis, and commentary by scholars, policymakers, and experts. The journal covers a wide range of topics, including diplomacy, security, trade, human rights, climate change, and technological advancements. It has historically been influential in shaping U.S. foreign policy and global discourse by providing a platform for leading thinkers and decision-makers. Notable contributors to Foreign Affairs include historical figures like Henry Kissinger, Madeleine Albright, and Francis Fukuyama. Its accessible yet scholarly approach appeals to both professionals in international relations and general readers interested in understanding the complexities of global affairs. The journal continues to serve as a key resource for understanding and debating world issues.
