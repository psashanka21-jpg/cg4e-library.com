The Bremen-town musicians

$2.00

The Bremen Town Musicians is a folktale collected by the Brothers Grimm about four aging animals—a donkey, a dog, a cat, and a rooster—who are no longer useful to their owners. Fearing they will be abandoned or killed, they decide to run away and become musicians in the town of Bremen. On their journey, they find a house occupied by robbers. To scare them away, they stand on top of each other and make a loud, terrifying noise. The robbers flee, thinking the house is haunted. The animals settle in the house, enjoying food and shelter. When a robber returns later, the animals attack him, and the robbers never come back. In the end, the animals live happily together in the house instead of reaching Bremen. The story highlights teamwork, resilience, and the idea that everyone can find a new purpose in life.
